Who It’s For

Designed to complement existing school systems — not replace them.

Schools

Send priority notifications, manage transport disruptions, notify parents during emergencies, and follow up on fees using your own Google Sheets.

Parents

Get urgent alerts about your child, transport updates, school notices, and know when to act — even when you are offline.

Transport Providers

Coordinate routes, receive pickup requests, identify students safely, and earn extra income when capacity allows.

How It Works

Simple. Secure. Built for real school environments.

1. School registers and customizes its profile.
2. School links its own Google Sheets (we don’t store data).
3. Parents and drivers connect to the school.
4. Priority notifications are sent via email, WhatsApp, and alerts.
5. Transport disruptions are handled faster.
